Our Story

About Exceptional Health and Human Services

Exceptional Health and Human Services was founded in 2022 with a mission to provide compassionate, reliable home care services that help individuals remain safe, comfortable, and supported in their own homes.

We proudly serve Miami Lakes and surrounding families with Medicaid-approved Homemaker and Companion Services, as well as affordable private pay care options. We understand that every client has unique needs, which is why we focus on personalized support, dignity, respect, and dependable care.

At Exceptional Health and Human Services, we believe care should feel personal. We are committed to treating every client like family and helping families feel confident in the support their loved ones receive.

What We Do

Our Services

Exceptional Health and Human Services offers non-medical in-home support designed to make everyday life easier and more comfortable.

Homemaker Services

Our Homemaker Services help clients maintain a safe, clean, and organized home environment. Services may include:

  • Light housekeeping
  • Meal preparation
  • Grocery shopping
  • Laundry

Companion Services

Our Companion Services provide emotional support and meaningful social interaction to improve quality of life. Services may include:

  • Emotional support
  • Social interaction
  • Assistance with appointments
  • Support with hobbies and daily routines

Private Pay Home Care Options

For families who may not qualify for Medicaid, we offer affordable private pay home care options. These services provide dependable, flexible support tailored to the needs of each client and family.
